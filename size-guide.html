Measurement Tips

📏

Height: Stand straight against a wall, measure from the floor to the top of your head without shoes.

⚖️

Weight: Weigh yourself in the morning in light clothing for the most accurate reading.

📐

Chest: Wrap a tape measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape level and snug but not tight.
